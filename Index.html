Air Quality Categories

Good Air Quality

Air quality is considered good when pollution levels are minimal. Breathing is not likely to be affected.

Medium Air Quality

Air quality is acceptable; however, some pollutants may be present. Sensitive individuals may experience discomfort.

Lower Medium Air Quality

Air quality is not ideal and may affect people with sensitivities. Consider taking precautions if you fall into sensitive groups.

Bad Air Quality

High pollution levels; everyone may start to experience health effects. Avoid prolonged outdoor activities.
